About Vidhya

Vidhya is a virtual assistant AI technology developed by Chinmoy Raj Deka mostly for Data Science Coaching Institute. It is designed using NLP (Natural Language Processing), CNN (Convolution Neural Network), Flask web framework and libraries for web scraping. It is capable of responsing to queries related to Data Science course as well as most of other queries. Users can make their queries related to the course by simply typing out them. Whereas queries related to other than the course should be made by typing "google" infront of them. The average waiting time for Vidhya's response is 3 seconds, and currently developer is working on decreasing the response time.
